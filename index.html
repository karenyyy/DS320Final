Oakland Crime Statistics Integration and Analysis

BY: Jiarong Ye, Yuan Meng, Zhixuan Yang

Introduction

Safety issue is always a focus for American citizens. It is a matter of happiness, quality of life and sense of safety. An explicit crime statistic can help people see and understand how safe/danger their living environment is. However, for most of the data we can reach, many of them are raw data, which takes a lot of time for normal people to interpret. Therefore, we decided to do an exploratory data analysis on the criminal data from Oakland, CA so that it is more self-explanatory, intuitive and meaningful.

Dataset Description

Oakland crime statistics dataset is a dataset hosted by the city of Oakland in California published on Kaggle. This dataset has six different CSV files, and each of them represents a single year from 2011 to 2016. Each file has similar columns, such as create and close times, area ID, and incident type, etc.
